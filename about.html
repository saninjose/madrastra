Erika Montoya and Carlos Bonil are artists and partners. They've started making collaborative works since 2016.

In 2022 they opened Galería Madrastra, an exhibition space in a warehouse in downtown Bogotá commited to the presentation and support of contemporary art and artists. Soon they are planning to move to Florida U.S.

Separately, they each have exhibitions as individual artists.
